<h4>Practice Description</h4>
    <p>Jeff has been in the private practice of law since 1977 in the State of Washington. In 1977-78 he was a law clerk to the Hon. Jack P. Schofield. Since 1978, the majority of his practice has concentrated on the representation of surety and fidelity insurers in the State of Washington and throughout the West. His practice focuses on surety bond defense work as well as subrogation and recovery work. Jeff has been very active with legislative and industry groups relating to the surety industry, has authored a number of articles dealing with the surety industry and has been a speaker at a number of seminars relating to surety issues. Along with Alex Friedrich, he formed Yusen & Friedrich in 1988.</p>
